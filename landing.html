Choose Your Program
Bulking Bulking is a fitness phase where individuals consume more calories than they burn to support muscle growth and increase body weight. It involves structured weightlifting and a diet rich in protein and carbohydrates to fuel muscle repair and growth, aiming to maximize muscle mass gains despite some fat gain.
Diet This program focuses on reducing body fat while avoiding muscle hypertrophy (growth). The primary goal is to achieve a leaner physique through a calorie-restricted diet, emphasizing fat loss. Exercise routines may include cardio and light resistance training to aid fat reduction, but not intense enough to significantly increase muscle size.
Maintenance A maintenance program aims to keep the body weight and composition stable. It involves consuming a balanced diet that matches the body's energy expenditure to maintain current weight and muscle mass. Regular physical activity is included to preserve fitness levels and overall health without significant changes in body composition.
Cutting Cutting is a term used in fitness and bodybuilding to describe a phase where individuals aim to reduce body fat This typically involves a combination of calorie restriction, cardiovascular exercise, and weight training. The goal of cutting is to achieve a leaner physique with defined muscles and reduced body fat percentage.
